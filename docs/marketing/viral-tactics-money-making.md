## Guardrails

- **Do:** Lead with real use cases; "potential" not "guaranteed"; cite x402, DePIN, market fit
- **Don't:** Promise specific returns; imply get-rich-quick; overstate adoption of x402 today
- **Reality check:** x402 and agent monetization are early; content should frame as "building toward" or "when x402 ships"
