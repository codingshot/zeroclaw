## 3. OpenClaw: Founder’s Anti-Crypto Stance

*Source: Rich by Coin, eand.co, crypto.news, CoinSpectator (Feb 2026).*

### Facts

- **Peter Steinberger** (OpenClaw creator) enforced a **blanket ban on all cryptocurrency mentions** in the project's Discord — including Bitcoin.
- **Scope:** All crypto references, even technical ones. Users have been banned for mentioning Bitcoin as a timing mechanism (e.g., block height) in a multi-agent benchmark.
- **Trigger:** $16M fake $CLAWD token scam during the Jan 2026 rebrand; harassment of Steinberger from traders who blamed him for not endorsing the token.
- **Policy:** "Zero-crypto" rule across Discord. When challenged, Steinberger acknowledged the blanket rule but has reinstated some banned users on appeal.

| Angle | One-liner |
