## Why Experiment with ZeroClaw

- **Edge-ready:** <5MB RAM, <10ms startup — runs on Pi Zero 2, $10 boards (OpenClaw needs >1GB, Mac Mini).
- **Secure by default:** Pairing, sandbox, encrypted secrets; no marketplace with 341 malicious skills.
- **Trait-driven:** Add Channel, Tool, Provider, Peripheral by implementing traits; clear CONTRIBUTING.
- **22+ providers:** Swap models; RAG in SQLite; no Pinecone lock-in.
- **Built by students:** Harvard, MIT, Sundai.Club communities.

## Do / Don’t

- **Do:** Invite experimentation; offer demo nights, workshops, office hours.
- **Don’t:** Spam; copy-paste identical DMs across communities.
- **Do:** Adapt language (English, Japanese, Chinese, French) for local hubs.
- **Don’t:** Over-claim; ZeroClaw is early, invite feedback.
- **Do:** Link repo, quick start, ideas board; keep it builder-friendly.
