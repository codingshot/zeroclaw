# Sensitive-Data Use Cases — Viral Angles for ZeroClaw

Use cases where sensitive information is the driver. Viral hook: "When your data can't touch the cloud." ZeroClaw = self-hosted, local, auditable — built for workloads where leakage is unacceptable.

Last updated: **February 18, 2026**.

---

## Why This Goes Viral

- **News cycle:** Cloud AI training on private data, API leaks, vendor breaches
- **Emotion:** People care intensely about health, legal, financial, identity
- **Contrast:** "Send it to the cloud" vs "Never leaves your device"
- **Trust:** "Your therapist notes. Your client files. Your trade secrets. Local."

**Guardrail:** Content describes use cases and angles only. No real sensitive data, no personal identifiers, no endorsements of specific compliance outcomes. Legal review recommended for regulated claims.

## Guardrails (Required)

- **Do:** Frame as architecture (self-hosted, local, auditable); cite general compliance concepts
- **Don't:** Guarantee HIPAA/SOC 2/FISMA compliance; promise legal outcomes; use real case data
- **Reality:** ZeroClaw provides infra; compliance is deployment + policy + legal review
- **AGENTS.md 9.1:** Never include real personal/sensitive data in docs, tests, or examples
