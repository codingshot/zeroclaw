# Viral Opportunities — Expanded

Additional viral ideas and content angles not fully covered in viral-tactics.md. Mix and match for campaigns, posts, and moments.

Last updated: **February 18, 2026**.

---

## 1. Speedrun & Competition Formats

| Idea | Format | Why viral |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **"Install speedrun"** | Timed: git clone → first message; leaderboard | Gamification; reproducible |
| **"Benchmark speedrun"** | Who gets lowest RAM/startup on given hardware? | Specs as sport |
| **"0 to robot in 10 min"** | Timed build: ZeroClaw + GPIO blink | Tangible; maker flex |
| **"Cold start olympics"** | Video: ZeroClaw vs X vs Y — side-by-side startup | Visual contrast |
| **"Smallest deploy"** | Who deploys to smallest/weirdest device? | Explorer appeal |
